Screen time is unavoidable, especially if you are a student or working in tech. But constant notifications and doom-scrolling absolutely wreck our attention spans and drain our energy. 

You don't need to throw your phone in a lake to get some peace. Try these mini detox habits:

* **No screens for the first 30 minutes of the day:** Don't let someone else's emergency or a random social media post set the tone for your morning.
* **Grayscale mode:** Go into your phone settings and turn the screen to black and white. It makes scrolling infinitely less addictive.
* **The "One Tab" rule:** When you are trying to focus, close every single browser tab except the one you actively need. 

Give your brain a break from the constant dopamine slot-machine. You will be amazed at how much clearer your head feels.
