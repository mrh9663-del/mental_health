Sometimes self-care feels like another chore on your to-do list. When you're running on empty, don't try to do it all. Just pick **one** thing from this menu:

**The "Appetizers"**
* **Hydrate:** Drink one full glass of water.
* **Sunlight:** Open a window or step outside for 60 seconds.
* **Change:** Put on a fresh pair of socks or a clean shirt.

**The "Main Courses"**
* **The Brain Dump:** Grab a piece of paper and write down everything stressing you out for 5 minutes, then throw the paper away. (Or use the "void" function on this site!)
* **Movement:** Do three slow stretches or walk to the end of the block and back.
* **Tidy:** Clean just one surface, maybe your nightstand or even your keyboard.

**The "Dessert"**
* **Unplug:** Put your phone in another room for 20 minutes.
* **Music:** Listen to one song that always makes you feel calm.

Don't wait for "motivation". Just pick one and start now.
