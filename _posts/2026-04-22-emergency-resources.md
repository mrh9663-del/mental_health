If you are in a crisis or just need someone to talk to right now, please reach out to these professional services. They are free, confidential, and available 24/7.

**Immediate Help**
* **Suicide & Crisis Lifeline:** Call or text **800 4673** (Available in the UAE).
* **Mental Health Helpline:** Call **800 111**, available 24/7
* **Emergency Line:** Call **999** for police emergency or **998** for medical emergencies.

**International Resources**
* **Befrienders Worldwide:** [Find a local helpline here](https://www.befrienders.org/)
* **International Association for Suicide Prevention:** [Find a Crisis Center](https://www.iasp.info/resources/Crisis_Centres/)

**Remember:** Reaching out for help isn't a sign of weakness, it’s an instinct of survival. Your life has value, and there are people who would love to listen.
