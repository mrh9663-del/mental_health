Welcome to **MoodTrack**! 👋

More often than what you'd like to acknowledge life gets overwhelming, and sometimes our brains feel like a browser with 100 tabs open. We built MoodTrack because we wanted a simple, zero-pressure way to hit the "pause" button and check in with ourselves. 

**What is this place?**
MoodTrack isn't social media. There are no likes, no followers, and absolutely no algorithms trying to keep you scrolling. It is just a private, digital journal for you to log how you are feeling, figure out what is impacting your mood, and spot patterns over time. 

**Privacy First**
Everything you log here stays right here. We use your browser's `localStorage` to save your check-ins, which means your data never leaves your device. No databases, no creepy tracking, just you and your thoughts.

Take a deep breath, log your mood, and remember: progress, not perfection. 💛
