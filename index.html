--- layout: single title: "Welcome to Your Mind Space" permalink: / --- Mental health is just as important as physical health, yet it often goes overlooked in our busy lives. Taking just a few moments a day to reflect on your emotions can drastically improve your self-awareness and emotional regulation. This platform is designed to give you a safe, private space to track your daily moods, understand the factors affecting your well-being, and let go of stress. ### 🌍 Why This Matters Mental health is a global priority. This project aligns directly with the **UN Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 3: Good Health and Well-being**, which aims to promote mental health and well-being for all ages. By building habits of mindfulness and self-reflection, we take a small step toward a healthier society. ### 🛠️ How to Use This Site * **Daily Check-in:** Log your mood on a scale of 1-10, note what factors influenced your day, and write a quick journal entry. * **History:** View your logged history and an interactive graph showing your mood trends over time. * **The Void:** A safe space to type out your frustrations and watch them disappear. Nothing here is saved. * **Posts:** (Coming Soon) A curated list of mental health resources and articles. Get started by navigating to the **Daily Check-in** at the top!
